Gujrat


Gujarat is a state with colourful traditions and a rich heritage. The state is home to several tourist attractions, which include the Rann of Kutch, Gir National Forest, etc. Apart from that, the state is also known for its colourful festivals, such as Navratri, Janmashtami, Rann Utsav, Shamlaji Melo and Uttarayan.
